Student Exchange (IISMA)
• IISMA is a scholarship scheme by the Government of Indonesia to
fund Indonesian students in mobility programs at top universities
abroad. Students can spend up to one semester at a partner
university abroad to study, learn about the culture of the
destination country, and do practical assignments to home skills.

Exchange
Course Policy
• Full study at partner university:
– Students who carry out international exposure activities at
partner campuses are only obliged to pay 10% of the
financing at ITS
• Full study at partner university & partial study at
partner university:
– Students who carry out international exposure activities at
partner campuses (on site) but also but also take some
courses at ITS (up to 6 credits - online) are obliged to pay
20% of the financing at ITS.
• Full study at ITS & partial study at partner university:
– Students who carry out partial international exposure
activities at partner campuses (online) and take full courses
at ITS (on site) are obliged to pay 100% of the financing at
